World's Best Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is a classic Italian dish that layers pasta, meat, cheese, and marinara sauce. It's perfect for family dinners or gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, ground beef, mozzarella cheese, and marinara sauce. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake for 45 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
  9. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
